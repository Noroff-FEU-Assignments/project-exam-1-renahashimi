Welcome to my blog

buta

[bo-ta] noun

Buta is a symbol in Azerbaijan and it means "fire" (bute-pute-buta).

"Buta Cuisine" is the blog where I share my food recipes. These recipes are from my culture, traditions and heritage.

Rena Hashimi

Living in a country other than your own home country can leave its mark after many years. By keeping traditions, cultures and heritage alive in my everyday life, I can take care of them and never forget them. One of the things that helps me remember my roots is the food I grew up with and now it's a pleasure to share these recipes with you.

"Welcome to a small piece of Azerbaijan ♥"
